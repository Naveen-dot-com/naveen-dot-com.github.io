Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Short version: Oshowani does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your conversations stay on your device. We do not track you.

1. Who We Are

Oshowani is a spiritual chat application developed by Naveen Dubey, an independent developer based in Varanasi, India. The app is available at https://naveen-dot-com.github.io.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at: n.k.dubey1997@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Oshowani does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location or IP address
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any demographic or profile information
  • Payment information of any kind

3. Conversation Data

When you type a message in Oshowani, your message is sent to Google Gemini API (operated by Google LLC) to generate a response. This transmission occurs over an encrypted HTTPS connection.

Important points:

  • We (Naveen Dubey / Oshowani) do not store your conversation on any server we control
  • Your conversation history is saved only on your own device using your browser's local storage
  • Clearing your browser/app data will permanently delete all conversation history
  • Google LLC may process your conversation text per their own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Local Storage

Oshowani stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your chat history and past conversations
  • Your language preference (e.g. English, Hindi)
  • Your theme preference (light or dark mode)

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

Oshowani uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Gemini API — for generating AI responses to your messages. Google's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media trackers.

6. Children's Privacy

Oshowani is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7. Data Security

All communication between your device and the Google Gemini API is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher). Since we do not store your data on any server, there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting your personal information.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal data about you, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser or app storage.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
